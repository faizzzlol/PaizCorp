SECTION 01
Browsing & Finding Items
01
Visit the Shop
Head to the Paiz Shop homepage. All available items are displayed in a grid. Items marked IN STOCK can be added to cart immediately.
02
Search & Filter
Use the search bar on the left to find items by name. You can also filter by category (Materials, Enchantments, Farming, Storage, etc.) or sort by price.
03
Check Stock
Each product card shows the current stock count. Items that are SOLD OUT cannot be added to cart — check back later or DM the shop owner on Discord.
SECTION 02
Adding to Cart & Checkout
04
Set Quantity & Add to Cart
Use the − / + buttons to set how many you want, then click ADD TO CART. The cart drawer will slide open from the right showing all your selected items.
05
Review Your Cart
Click the cart icon (top right) anytime to open the cart drawer. You can adjust quantities or remove items. Your total is shown in Diamonds (◆) at the bottom.
💡 TIP: You can add multiple different items to one order — no need to check out separately for each item.
06
Proceed to Checkout
Click CHECKOUT at the bottom of the cart. A checkout modal will appear asking for your details.
07
Fill in Your Details
You'll need to provide:
• Minecraft IGN — your in-game username
• Discord username — so we can contact you
• Delivery preference — Pickup or Delivery
• Location — your coordinates if choosing delivery (X Y Z format)
💡 DELIVERY: Choose Pickup to collect from the shop, or Delivery to have items brought to your location in-game.
08
Place Your Order
Click PLACE ORDER. Your order is automatically sent to our Discord channel as a notification. You'll get a success confirmation with your Order ID — save it!
📋 Order ID: Every order gets a unique ID like ORD-IIVC4J. You'll need this to track your order status on the Order History page.
SECTION 03
Tracking Your Order
09
Visit Order History
Go to the Order History page to see all orders you've placed on this device. Your orders are saved to your browser automatically.
10
Check Live Status
The order history page fetches the latest status directly from the shop. Status updates in real-time as the shop owner processes your order.
⏳ PENDING — Order received, waiting to be confirmed
✅ CONFIRMED — Shop owner confirmed, preparing your items
📦 DELIVERED — Items delivered or ready for pickup
❌ CANCELLED — Order was cancelled (check Discord for reason)
SECTION 04
