How to Use the Website

Welcome to Paiz Shop! Follow these instructions to make the most of our website:

  1. Browsing Items: Explore the available items listed on the homepage.
  2. Checking Stock: Each item shows its available stock. If the item is out of stock, the "Buy Now" button will be disabled, and a "Sold Out" message will appear.
  3. Adding Items to Cart: Enter the quantity you want and click the "Buy Now" button. If stock is available, the item will be added to your cart, and you’ll be redirected to the checkout page.
  4. Reviewing Your Cart: On the checkout page, review the items in your cart along with the subtotal, delivery fee, and tax.
  5. Choosing Delivery or Pickup: During checkout, select whether you want your order delivered or if you’ll pick it up. If you choose delivery, you’ll need to provide your delivery coordinates (X, Y, Z).
  6. Calculating Delivery Fees: The delivery fee is calculated at 1 diamond per 1000 blocks based on the distance from the shop’s location (X: 5894, Y: 69, Z: 5321) to your delivery coordinates.
  7. Viewing Delivery Fee Details: Hover over the info icon next to the delivery fee to view more details.
  8. Placing Your Order: Once all details are reviewed, click the "Place Order" button to complete your purchase. Your order details will be sent to a Discord channel, including your contact information.
  9. Tracking Orders: You can view your Order History and Ongoing Orders on the Order History page, where all your past and current orders are saved.
  10. Dark Mode: Toggle between dark and light themes using the switch in the top-right corner of the page. Your preference will be saved for future visits.
  11. Contact Us: If you have any questions or need assistance, visit our Discord server for support.
