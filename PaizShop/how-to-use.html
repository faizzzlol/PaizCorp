Welcome to Paiz Shop!

Follow these instructions to shop for your favorite Minecraft items:

1. Browse the Shop

Visit our home page to see the list of items available for purchase. Each item has a description and a current stock level.

2. Select Your Items

Use the quantity input field to choose the number of items you want. If the item is in stock, you can proceed to purchase.

3. Buy an Item

  • Click the "Buy Now" button next to the item you want to purchase.
  • You will be redirected to the Checkout page.

4. Checkout

On the checkout page:

  • Enter your Minecraft Name (required).
  • Select either Pickup or Delivery.
  • If you choose Delivery, provide your coordinates (X, Y, Z).
  • Review your order summary, which includes item details, subtotal, delivery fee (if applicable), tax, and total amount.
  • Click the Checkout button to finalize your purchase.

5. Order Confirmation

Your order details will be sent to a Discord channel and also to your Discord account (if you provided your Discord username during checkout). Please check your Discord messages for confirmation.
