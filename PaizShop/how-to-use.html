How to Use the Website

Welcome to Paiz Shop! Follow these instructions to buy items:

  1. Browse the items listed on the homepage.
  2. Select the quantity you want for each item.
  3. Click the "Buy Now" button to add the item to your cart.
  4. Review your cart and proceed to checkout.
  5. Choose between delivery or pickup options during checkout.
  6. Complete the payment process and receive a confirmation message.

How to Buy Items

1. Navigate to the item you wish to purchase.

2. Enter the desired quantity in the input field.

3. Click the "Buy Now" button to proceed.

4. Follow the checkout instructions to complete your purchase.
