SYNOPSIS

In the world of The Legend of Legiona, three players stand at the heart of a nation built block by block. When their world is threatened, they must fight — not just as players, but as a people.

The first ever full-length film produced by PaizProductions, shot entirely inside Minecraft on the Skyxion: Ragnarok server. A story of loyalty, war, and survival.
